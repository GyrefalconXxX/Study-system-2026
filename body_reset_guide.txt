--- THE BODY RESET GUIDE ---
1. FORWARD FOLD: Stand or sit, hang your head and torso over your knees. 
   Breathe into your lower back. (Counteracts sitting)

2. WALL CHEST STRETCH: Arm against a doorframe, turn away gently. 
   (Counteracts writing/typing hunch)

3. THE SPHINX: Lie on your belly, prop up on elbows, look forward. 
   (Counteracts looking down at papers)

4. BELLY BREATHING: 5 mins slow breathing. 
   (Lowers cortisol for deeper sleep/fasting)
